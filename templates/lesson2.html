Lesson 2: Tamil Pronunciation

Tamil pronunciation can be a bit tricky for native English speakers. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • The Tamil alphabet is pronounced phonetically, which means that each letter has a specific sound.
  • Tamil words are typically stressed on the first syllable.
  • Tamil has a number of sounds that do not exist in English, such as the retroflex consonants (e.g., ட், ண், ற்).
